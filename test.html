Refund Policy

1. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must request it within 30 days from the date of purchase. After 30 days, we won't be able to offer you a refund for any reason.

2. Valid Reasons for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • The course content did not meet your expectations.
  • You encountered technical issues that prevented you from accessing the course.
  • Duplicate purchase due to a technical error.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Some items are non-refundable, including but not limited to:

  • Downloadable course materials.
  • Courses that have been completed or partially completed.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at learniversehelp@gmail.com .Please include your order details and the reason for your refund request.

5. Processing Refunds

Once your refund request is received and approved, we will process the refund within 2 days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used during the purchase.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our refund policy, please feel free to contact us at learniversehelp@gmail.com or Contact us.
